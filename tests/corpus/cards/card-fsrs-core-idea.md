# Card: FSRS core idea

**Q**: What does FSRS do differently from SM-2?

**A**: FSRS fits a **17-parameter power-law memory model** on
population review data, outputting per-card intervals that target 90%
retrievability. SM-2 uses a fixed formula driven by a per-card "ease
factor" reported by the user. Result: FSRS achieves ~30% lower review
workload at equal retention.
